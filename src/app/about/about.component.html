To provide residential facilities in the form of hostel accommodation to the maximum number of students is in the priority of Guru Jambheshwar University of Science & Technology. University provides hostel accommodation to all girls students who make a request for it. There are nine hostels, four for boys and four for girls in the university, which can house more than 2000 students. In addition to this One hostel (Working Woman Hostel) is exclusively being constructed for Ph.D. Research scholar and Working Woman in the University. Each hostel is provided with indoor and outdoor game facilities by the hostel administration and as well as by the sports department. Medical facilities are provided by the university Health Centre to the entire resident along with 24 hours ambulance facility. All the hostels are provided with 24 hours Internet connectivity through physical and wireless mode.

Aryabhatt Sadan
BH II- BOY's HOSTEL NO. II

Boys Hostel No. II provides residential accommodation to the Post Graduate students and research scholars. There are 120 rooms in the hostel, having the capacity of 240 students. Among the infrastructural facilities the hostel has well equipped mess kitchen & dining hall, which includes the equipments like doughkneeder, potato peeler, stainless steel dining tables, worktables, and racks for different purposes. Cold storage unit is an important component among the kitchen items. Water purifiers are functional in the mess as well as in different blocks of hostel. To maintain the hygienic conditions in mess kitchen & hall, there is proper provision of cleanliness and ventilation. In the facilities hostel provides study chairs and tables to all students. The hostel also provides three-seater and two-seater iron chairs for making a seating arrangement in the dinning hall, common room as well as in the lawns of hostel. As mentioned earlier the hostel is provided 24 hours Internet connectivity through physical and wire less mode. Reading material in the form of NEWS papers and magazines are available in the hostel. There is Contractual system of mess in the hostel. Students manage the affairs of mess in consultancy with the warden. There is a hostel managing committee of students representing the students of all departments, which looks after the mess activities including the kitchen materials. The committee on rotation basis also facilitates the activities related to cleanliness, discipline, water saving, gardening, and controlling of food wastage etc. The hostel has organized no. of activities like chess tournament, cultural function VIBHOR : INSPIRING Youth, Seminar etc . In the programme various experts of the field covered the activities related to work shops and lecturers on Yoga and Health, Life Skills, Stress Management, Alcohol and Drug-De-addiction and Nutrition and Health. The basic objectives of the programme were to develop the life skills among students, to sensitize them towards their health and health behaviour, to cultivate the attitude of optimism and positivity and to inculcate the value of self-extension.

BH III- BOY's HOSTEL NO. III

Boys hostel no. III came in existence in year 2006 with a set of 96 rooms. It is a three story building. Each floor have 32 (Thirty Two) rooms with 36 toilets and 27 bathrooms. Currently, the students residing in the hostel are doing various Under-Graduate and Post-Graduate courses. In the hostel, there is one Common Room, and one recreation hall. In the common room a colour LCD is installed, and in recreation room Carom Board, Table-Tennis, Chess is placed for the recreation of the residents of the hostel. Five national level newspapers are available in the hostel for reading. In Recreation room many internet connecting/ accessing plugs are installed to enable students to get in touch with Internet. Keeping in view the comfortness of the student's decent sitting arrangement have been made by placing three seater benches in both the Common Room and recreation room. Wi-Fi system has also been installed in the hostel premises. To make the energy efficient, CFL tube lights have been installed in the entire hostel premises. Solar Water Heater are also installed to provide hot water to the residents. Each floor is having one water-cooler with water-purifier to provide cold water to the residents in the summer season.
